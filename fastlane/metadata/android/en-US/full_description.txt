<p><i>Quick Settings</i> is a powerful Android app that brings essential system controls right to your notification shade with beautiful, modern tiles. Get one-tap access to frequently used functions without navigating through multiple menus.</p><p>We use tile services and accessibility service api to provide seamless integration with Android's Quick Settings panel, giving you instant control over your device's most important features.</p><p>How can this app help you? Well here are the main features of this app:</p><ul><li>Volume Control tile for quick audio management with support for multiple audio streams</li><li>Screenshot tile for instant screen capture without root access (Android 9.0+)</li><li>Lock Screen tile for one-tap device locking (Android 9.0+)</li><li>Power Menu tile for quick access to restart, shutdown, and reboot options</li><li>Developer Options tile for instant access to developer settings and debugging tools</li><li>Autostart Manager tile for controlling app autostart permissions in Chinese ROMs</li></ul><p>Key benefits of using Quick Settings:</p><ul><li>All tiles are directly accessible from your notification shade</li><li>Beautiful modern icons with consistent design language</li><li>Minimal permissions required - only what's necessary for functionality</li><li>Direct boot aware - works on encrypted devices</li><li>Optimized for performance and battery efficiency</li></ul><p>Privacy and Security:</p><ul><li>No internet connection required - works completely offline</li><li>No data collection or analytics</li><li>Open source and transparent code</li><li>Accessibility permission is optional and only used for advanced features</li></ul><p>Compatibility:</p><ul><li>Requires Android 7.0 (API 24) and above</li><li>Some features require Android 9.0+ for enhanced functionality</li><li>Works on all Android devices supporting Quick Settings</li></ul><p>Installation is simple - just add the tiles you want to your Quick Settings panel and enjoy instant access to your favorite features!</p>
